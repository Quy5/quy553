Every day is better with hash browns!

Welcome to Odd Shop! We’re a vibrant Clifton café, serving great coffee, food and vibes to the people of Bristol. Pop in today or take a look at our online menus (p.s. You won’t regret ordering our famous hash browns!)

We loooove coffee, brunch and our customers!

Our friendly staff are here to welcome you with open arms, always ready with a warm smile and a genuine conversation. It’s the kind of place where you’re greeted by name, where your favourite drink is remembered, and where you feel part of something special.

We’re more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee – we’re your home away from home. Whether you’re catching up with friends, getting some work done, or just enjoying a moment to yourself, we’ve created a cosy corner where everyone’s welcome – including your furry friends!.

Locally sourced, freshly served

We locally source ingredients, fresh from our community to your plate. Bristol is full of independent-run businesses with great products, which we are proud to use in our Clifton café, making delicious dishes.

Whether you’re after brekkie, brunch, lunch or a sweet treat, we’ve got something to satisfy your craving. Our talented chefs are always whipping up exciting new dishes, blending creativity with the freshest ingredients, your tastebuds will be blown away!
